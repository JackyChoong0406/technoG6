Minesweeper Instructions

  • Click a cell to reveal it.
  • Right-click to place or remove a flag.
  • The number shows how many mines are around that cell.
  • You have 30 seconds to score as much as possible.
  • Your first click will not end the game, even if it lands on a mine.
  • Reach the target score to unlock the next level.

Click start when ready.
